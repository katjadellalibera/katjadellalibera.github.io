About Me

I'm Katja Della Libera, a Ph.D. Candidate in the department of Ecology & Evolution at the University of Chicago, researching with the Blekhman lab.

My interest is in the significance of ecological community dynamics in human health and disease. Specifically, I am creating metabolic models of the gut microbiome and host cells in IBD and colorectal cancer.
